Class Schedules

Mathematics

Topic Subtopics
Numbers Introduction to Numbers, Place Value (Ones, Tens, Hundreds), Comparison and Ordering of Numbers
Addition and Subtraction Basic Addition and Subtraction, Word Problems Involving Addition and Subtraction
Multiplication Introduction to Multiplication, Multiplication Tables (1 to 10), Word Problems on Multiplication
Division Introduction to Division, Division Facts and Simple Word Problems
Fractions Understanding Fractions (Half, Quarter), Simple Operations with Fractions
Measurement Measuring Length, Weight, and Capacity, Introduction to Time (Hours and Minutes)
Geometry Basic Shapes (Circle, Triangle, Square, Rectangle), Properties of Shapes
Data Handling Collecting Data, Simple Bar Graphs and Pictographs

Science

Physical Science Subtopics
Living and Non-Living Things Characteristics of Living Things, Difference Between Living and Non-Living Things
Plants and Animals Parts of a Plant and Their Functions, Types of Animals (Domestic, Wild)
Materials Different Types of Materials (Wood, Metal, Plastic), Properties of Materials
Energy Introduction to Energy Sources (Sun, Wind), Simple Machines (Lever, Pulley)

Biological Science

Topic Subtopics
Human Body Basic Body Parts and Their Functions, Importance of Hygiene
Health and Nutrition Balanced Diet and Nutrition, Healthy Habits and Exercise

Social Studies

History Subtopics
Our Heritage Introduction to Indian Culture and Heritage, Important Festivals of India
Famous Personalities Short Stories of Great Indian Leaders and Freedom Fighters
Geography Subtopics
Our Planet Introduction to Earth and its Features, Basic Map Skills (Understanding Directions)
Weather and Seasons Types of Weather, Seasons and Their Characteristics
Political Science Subtopics
Community and Neighbourhood Understanding Our Community, Roles of Different People in a Community
Basic Rights and Responsibilities Introduction to Rights and Responsibilities of Children

English

Topic Subtopics
Literature Stories and Poems, Understanding Characters and Themes
Grammar Basic Parts of Speech (Nouns, Verbs, Adjectives), Simple Sentences, Punctuation (Full Stops, Question Marks)
Writing Skills Writing Short Sentences and Paragraphs, Descriptive Writing

Second Language

Language Subtopics
Telugu / Hindi Basic Prose and Poetry from prescribed textbooks, Introduction to Vocabulary and Simple Grammar, Writing Simple Sentences and Short Paragraphs
