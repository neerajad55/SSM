About Us

Our Mission

At Sunrise High School, our mission is to provide a holistic and high-quality education that nurtures the intellectual, social, and emotional development of our students, preparing them for success in a rapidly changing world.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every student has access to excellent education, empowering them to reach their full potential and contribute positively to society.

Core Values

  • Excellence: We strive for the highest standards in everything we do.
  • Integrity: We promote honesty, ethics, and transparency.
  • Respect: We value diversity and treat everyone with dignity.
  • Community: We foster a sense of belonging and collaboration.
  • Innovation: We encourage creativity and critical thinking to solve real-world challenges.

Our History

Sunrise High School was founded in 1985 with the goal of providing quality education to students in the Springfield area. Over the past three decades, we have grown from a small school with just 100 students to a thriving community of over 1,000 learners. Our school has consistently ranked among the top schools in the region, thanks to our dedicated staff and innovative educational programs.
