Class Schedules

Mathematics

Topic Subtopics
Large Numbers Place Value and Face Value, Comparison of Numbers, Ordering and Rounding Numbers
Operations on Numbers Addition and Subtraction of Large Numbers, Multiplication and Division, Word Problems on Operations
Fractions Introduction to Fractions, Types of Fractions (Proper, Improper, Mixed), Operations on Fractions (Addition, Subtraction)
Decimals Introduction to Decimals, Comparison of Decimals, Operations on Decimals (Addition, Subtraction)
Measurement Units of Measurement (Length, Mass, Capacity), Converting Units, Perimeter and Area of Basic Shapes
Geometry Basic Geometric Shapes (Triangles, Squares, Rectangles, Circles), Properties of Shapes, Introduction to Angles
Data Handling Collecting and Organizing Data, Pictographs and Bar Graphs, Simple Analysis of Data

Science

Physical Science Subtopics
Matter and Materials States of Matter (Solid, Liquid, Gas), Properties of Materials
Force and Motion Types of Forces, Effects of Force, Simple Machines (Levers, Pulleys)
Energy Different Forms of Energy (Kinetic, Potential), Sources of Energy, Importance of Energy Conservation
Air and Water Composition of Air, Properties of Water, Water Cycle
Plants and Animals Parts of Plants and Their Functions, Classification of Animals, Habitats and Adaptations

Biological Science

Topic Subtopics
Life Processes Nutrition, Respiration, and Reproduction in Plants and Animals, Importance of Life Processes
Health and Hygiene Importance of Health, Healthy Habits and Personal Hygiene, Common Diseases and Their Prevention

Social Studies

History Subtopics
Early Humans and Their Culture Hunter-Gatherers, Development of Agriculture
Civilizations Indus Valley Civilization, Ancient Indian Empires
Freedom Struggle Key Events Leading to Indian Independence, Important Leaders and Their Contributions
Geography Subtopics
The Earth and Its Features Structure of the Earth, Major Landforms (Mountains, Rivers, Plains)
Maps Importance of Maps, Reading and Understanding Maps
Weather and Climate Factors Affecting Weather, Types of Climate
Political Science Subtopics
Understanding Government Role of Government, Types of Government (Democracy, Monarchy)
Rights and Responsibilities Fundamental Rights, Duties of Citizens

English

Topic Subtopics
Literature Prose and Poetry, Understanding Themes and Characters
Grammar Parts of Speech, Simple Sentences, Punctuation and Capitalization, Writing Short Essays and Letters

Second Language

Language Subtopics
Telugu / Hindi Prose and Poetry from prescribed textbooks, Basic Grammar and Vocabulary, Writing Simple Sentences and Compositions
