School Syllabus

Subject Topics
Mathematics
  • Numbers
    • Introduction to Numbers (1 to 100)
    • Counting and Writing Numbers
    • Place Value (Ones and Tens)
  • Basic Operations
    • Introduction to Addition (Simple Sums)
    • Introduction to Subtraction (Simple Differences)
    • Word Problems Involving Addition and Subtraction
  • Shapes and Patterns
    • Identifying Basic Shapes (Circle, Square, Triangle, Rectangle)
    • Recognizing Patterns (Repeating Patterns)
  • Measurement
    • Understanding Length (Longer and Shorter)
    • Introduction to Weight (Heavier and Lighter)
    • Understanding Time (Days of the Week, Clock Basics)
  • Data Handling
    • Collecting Simple Data
    • Understanding and Interpreting Pictographs
Science
  • Physical Science
    • Living and Non-Living Things
      • Characteristics of Living Things
      • Differences Between Living and Non-Living Things
    • Plants and Animals
      • Introduction to Plants (Parts of a Plant)
      • Introduction to Animals (Farm Animals and Wild Animals)
    • Materials Around Us
      • Identifying Common Materials (Wood, Metal, Plastic)
      • Basic Properties of Materials (Soft, Hard, Rough, Smooth)
  • Biological Science
    • The Human Body
      • Basic Body Parts (Head, Arms, Legs, etc.)
      • Importance of Hygiene (Washing Hands, Bathing)
    • Health and Nutrition
      • Understanding Healthy Foods
      • Importance of Exercise and Play
Social Studies
  • History
    • My Family
      • Importance of Family
      • Different Types of Families
    • Celebrations and Festivals
      • Major Festivals Celebrated in India
      • Cultural Significance of Festivals
  • Geography
    • Our Earth
      • Basic Features of the Earth (Land, Water)
      • Introduction to Maps (Understanding Directions)
    • Weather and Seasons
      • Types of Weather (Sunny, Rainy, Cloudy)
      • Different Seasons (Summer, Winter, Monsoon)
  • Political Science
    • Community and Neighbourhood
      • Understanding the Community
      • Importance of Neighbours
    • Basic Rights and Responsibilities
      • Understanding the Rights of Children
      • Responsibilities Towards Family and Society
English
  • Literature
    • Stories and Poems (specific chapters from textbooks)
    • Understanding Characters and Themes
  • Grammar
    • Introduction to Nouns and Verbs
    • Simple Sentences (Constructing Basic Sentences)
    • Punctuation (Full Stops, Question Marks)
  • Writing Skills
    • Writing Simple Sentences
    • Descriptive Writing About Family and Friends
Second Language (Telugu / Hindi)
  • Basic Prose and Poetry from prescribed textbooks
  • Introduction to Vocabulary and Simple Grammar
  • Writing Simple Sentences
