Class Schedules

Mathematics

Topic Subtopics
Numbers Introduction to Numbers, Place Value and Face Value, Comparison and Ordering of Numbers
Operations on Numbers Addition and Subtraction of Large Numbers, Word Problems on Addition and Subtraction
Multiplication and Division Multiplication Facts and Properties, Long Division, Word Problems on Multiplication and Division
Fractions Understanding Fractions (Proper and Improper), Addition and Subtraction of Fractions
Measurement Units of Measurement (Length, Mass, Capacity), Introduction to Perimeter and Area of Simple Shapes
Geometry Basic Geometric Shapes (Circle, Triangle, Square, Rectangle), Properties of Shapes
Data Handling Collecting and Organizing Data, Pictographs, Simple Graphs

Science

Physical Science Subtopics
Matter States of Matter (Solid, Liquid, Gas), Properties of Matter
Force and Motion Introduction to Force, Simple Machines (Lever, Pulley)
Energy Sources of Energy (Renewable and Non-renewable), Forms of Energy (Light, Heat, Sound)
Natural Resources Types of Natural Resources, Conservation of Natural Resources

Biological Science

Topic Subtopics
Plants Parts of a Plant and Their Functions, Types of Plants (Herbs, Shrubs, Trees)
Animals Classification of Animals, Habitats of Animals
Health and Hygiene Importance of Health, Healthy Eating and Personal Hygiene

Social Studies

History Subtopics
The Story of Our Past Early Humans and Their Lifestyle, Development of Agriculture

Geography Subtopics
Our Earth Basic Structure of the Earth, Landforms: Mountains, Hills, Valleys, and Plains
Weather and Climate Understanding Weather, Importance of Climate
Political Science Subtopics
Understanding Government Role of Government in Society, Types of Government
Rights and Responsibilities Importance of Fundamental Rights, Duties of Citizens

English

Topic Subtopics
Literature Stories and Poems, Understanding Characters and Themes
Grammar Parts of Speech (Nouns, Verbs, Adjectives), Simple Sentences and Punctuation, Writing Short Essays and Descriptions

Second Language

Language Subtopics
Telugu / Hindi Prose and Poetry from prescribed textbooks, Basic Grammar and Vocabulary, Writing Simple Sentences and Short Essays
